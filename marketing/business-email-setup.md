## Step 2 — Set Up Google Workspace (Best Option)

Google Workspace gives you Gmail with your custom domain.
Cost: $6/month (Starter plan — 1 user, 30GB storage)

1. Go to: https://workspace.google.com
2. Click "Get Started"
3. Enter your domain (edziban.com)
4. Follow the setup — Google walks you through it step by step
5. You'll verify domain ownership by adding a DNS record (Google explains this)

Result: You get **orders@edziban.com** that works exactly like regular Gmail.

**Recommended email addresses to create:**
- orders@edziban.com  ← put this on the website for customers
- hello@edziban.com   ← general inquiries
- (admin@edziban.com is already used in the site as the admin login)

## Free Alternative — Zoho Mail

If you don't want to pay $6/month yet:

1. Go to: https://zoho.com/mail
2. Sign up for the free plan (1 user, 5GB)
3. Add your domain and verify it
4. Create orders@edziban.com for free

Downside: Not as polished as Gmail. But it works fine to start.
