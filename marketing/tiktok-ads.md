## POSTING TIPS

- Post 3–4x per week minimum when starting out
- Use these hashtags every post:
  #GhanaianFood #BostonGhana #GhanaFood #AfricanCatering #JollofRice
  #GhanaBoston #BostonFoodie #GhanaianCatering #Waakye #AfricanFood

- Best posting times: 7–9pm weekdays, 12–2pm weekends

- Film in good lighting. Natural window light works. You don't need a ring light.

- TikTok profile bio should say:
  "Authentic Ghanaian catering, Boston area.
   Funerals | Weddings | Naming Ceremonies | Parties
   Orders: edziban.com"

- Pin your best performing ad to the top of your profile

---

## WHAT TO FILM (no studio needed)

1. Yourself talking to camera (phone propped up, good light)
2. The food itself — if you make a test batch, film it on a clean surface
3. If you attend an event — get footage of the full spread laid out
4. Show yourself packing orders into containers / trays (builds trust)
